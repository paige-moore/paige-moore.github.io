    Research Neuroplasticity 

    Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

    When we learn, we form new pathways in the brain. Each new lesson has the potential to connect new neurons and change our brain’s default mode of operation. It is through this sort of learning that we may be able to figure out how to purposefully rewire the brain. Understanding that the brain can be rewired gives people the confidence to go out and learn new things, because it is never to late.

    Research Growth Mindset

    Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

    Growth mindset believes that person can get smarter, better, or more skilled at something through sustained effort—which is exactly what neuroplasticity tells us. You might say that a growth mindset is simply accepting the idea of neuroplasticity on a broad level!
